A message from the heart

Baby, I know you're missing me…

My dearest Gauri,

I know right now you're having a bad mood — and I know exactly why. You're missing me. And I want you to know: I am missing you too, so much.

You say "I love you more" so often, and I never debate you on it. Not because I don't have anything to say — but because I want to let you feel that. The way you love me is something I have never, in my entire life, truly wanted from anyone else. Of all the people who have ever cared for me, you are the only one I truly wanted to be loved by like this.

I totally, completely admire how you love me. It makes me feel something I genuinely do not have words for. You make me so happy, Gauri — and I'm sorry I can't always explain it properly, because the feeling is bigger than any sentence I could build.

But please don't ever read my silence as love that is less. I love you more than anything. I have never been this sure about anyone. You are certain to me — like breathing, like morning, like something I would never put in question.

You are my wife, Gauri. And I will love you like this — every single day, growing a little more each morning — for every day I have left.

You are the gem I found. The rarest, most beautiful one. And I will never stop being grateful that I get to keep you.

Always yours,Your Buddhu 💕
